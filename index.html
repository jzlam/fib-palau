Palau Foreign Investment Board

The Foreign Investment Board is a regulatory body within the executive branch of the national government of the Republic of Palau. Its chartered function is to review applications for Foreign Investment Approval Certificates (FIAC's), and to enforce compliance with the terms and conditions of the investment therein.

The FIB is chartered by the Foreign Investment Act, ยง 104.
