Contact the FIB


The Foreign Investment Board is located adjacent to the Satellite Office of the President in Meyuns, Palau.

Preliminary and current investor inquiries are handled during normal business hours. Please review our resources for new and current Investors to familiarize yourself with our required guidelines.
